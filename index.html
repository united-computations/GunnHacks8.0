About

The eighth iteration of GunnHacks, Gunn High School's annual hackathon.

Join us from February 5th to 6th from 2 PM to 2 PM for a virtual, 24 hour high school hackathon. Work in teams of up to four to create something amazing. We welcome any precollege student with an interest in technology!

Registration is now open! Register for GunnHacks here. All hackers are expected to follow MLH's Code of Conduct.
